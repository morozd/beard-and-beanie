What I do

I specialize in making lightweight, mobile ready, and easily maintainable websites that are cheap or even free to host. My great-grandfather was sign painter and I think of designing for the web a lot like he thought of sign painting: I'm making something that is going to be a buisness's face; that first point of contact; a transmitter of important information; a hook to draw people in; a "once upon a time." I hand craft each site to your particular needs.

Websites aren't the only thing that I do, however! Need that new printer installed, or a network setup? I have got you covered. Do you want to set up a new e-mail address using a custom domain name? I can do that too. Would you like to catologe and digitize some old photos, or how about build a database of clients, or an archive of family heirlooms? I would love to help you with all of these things and more—think of me as your digital handyman.
